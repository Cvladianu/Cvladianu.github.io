Scenarios

Having created the Personas, we then proceeded to create scenarios, in order to understand how different users would interact with our Web Platform.[4]



Scenario 1

Adam enjoys developing small videogames as a hobby. Throughout the years, he has developed about a dozen of games, but never managed to reach to many players, as he just published them in his own blog.


Upon learning about Fizzyo, Adam is determined to release his games on this framework, as it would not only reach out to users, but help do good. For this task, he will expect thorough documentation and easy to follow explanations on how to make his existing library, Fizzyo compatible.




Scenario 2

Henry has already successfully launched a couple of titles on the Fizzyo framework, and is looking to make some more games. For this, he would like to see data on the success of each game on the platform.


By learning about which games were the most played, which aided the most for the actual Fizzyo treatment, and other similar data, he will have a better idea on what to do next. For Henry, the value of these tools is immeasurable.
